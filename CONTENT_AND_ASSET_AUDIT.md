# Coach V5 Content and Asset Audit

## Result

- 38 exercises are present in `data/equipment.js`.
- Every exercise has a list thumbnail and an in-use image: 76 machine PNGs total.
- Every exercise has a category, type, muscle mapping, defaults, four instructions, five common mistakes, and a coach tip.
- All 76 machine PNGs have an alpha channel and transparent corners.
- All 38 list thumbnails received a second edge-connected matte cleanup to remove pale background fragments while preserving enclosed white and silver machine details.
- No referenced exercise or machine image is missing.

## Catalog coverage

- Strength machines: leg press, chest press, lat pulldown, seated row, shoulder press, leg extension, hip abductor, ab crunch, back extension, calf raise, glute kickback, hack squat, assisted pull-up, chest fly.
- Cable machines: cable tower, cable hip abduction, cable biceps bar, cable triceps bar, cable rope.
- Free weights and stations: Smith machine, dumbbells, barbell, kettlebell, medicine ball, lateral raise, bicep curl station, squat rack, bench press, incline press.
- Cardio and functional: treadmill, elliptical, stair climber, jump rope, battle ropes, box jump, chin-up, balance ball, full body stretch.

## Remaining content decision

The current catalog is internally complete. The only open question is scope: if Coach should mirror a specific gym location, compare this list against that location's physical equipment inventory before final release.
